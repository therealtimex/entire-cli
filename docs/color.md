# Blue

Blue is a primary color in the RGB color model. It is often associated with the sky, the sea, and feelings of calmness and serenity.

In art and design, blue can represent:
- Trust
- Loyalty
- Wisdom
- Confidence
- Intelligence

Different shades of blue include:
- Sky blue
- Navy blue
- Royal blue
- Cyan
- Teal
- Azure

# Red

Red is a primary color, evoking strong emotions such as passion, love, anger, and danger. It is often used to signify importance, warning, or excitement.

In various cultures, red can symbolize:
- Energy
- War
- Courage
- Sacrifice

Different shades of red include:
- Scarlet
- Crimson
- Ruby
- Maroon
- Burgundy
